# Privacy Policy

**TourGuideNow** ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the TourGuideNow mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

Our app collects:
- **Location data** — to show nearby places, calculate distances, and provide navigation
- **Email address** — for account creation, password recovery, and communication
- **User preferences** — onboarding answers to personalize your experience
- **Activity data** — completed trips, ratings, and reviews you provide

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for marketing purposes
- Track you outside the app

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. We use:
- **Firebase Authentication** — for secure login
- **MongoDB Atlas** — for user profiles and activity data
- **Cloudinary** — for image and audio storage

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses:
- **Google Maps API** — to display location data
- **Firebase** — for authentication and real-time features
- **Mapbox** — for mapping functionality

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

## Data Retention

- User account data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time
- Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date below.
